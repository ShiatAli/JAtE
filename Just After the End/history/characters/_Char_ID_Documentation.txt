For particular characters feel free to put them anywhere under 010000, as that is all totally unused space going off this system.

#The first two digits of the ID tell you which Empire it is in, alphabetically from 1 to 20. The third digit tells you what kingdom under that empire, the fourth digit is duchy, then the fifth and sixth digits are for all the characters under that duchy. I doubt that we will have more than even one duchy with 99 characters in it, but if so just give that duchy another data bit in the fourth digit, and make sure to mark that down in a comment in the appropriate place.
